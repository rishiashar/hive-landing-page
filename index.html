What is Hive?

Hive is a student led community at the University of Toronto for people who care about design, technology, creativity, and building things together. We bring students into one space to connect, learn, share ideas, and grow through events, workshops, conversations, and collaborations.

Hive was created to make the student experience feel more connected and inspiring. It is a place where curious people can meet others, explore new interests, and be part of a community that values experimentation, learning, and making.

At Hive, learning is a two way experience. It is not just about listening or attending. It is about sharing what you know, learning from others, and growing together as a community.

Whether you are into UX, product design, coding, creative tech, branding, or just want to meet ambitious and creative people, Hive is a space for you.
